Our Mission

VDTC is a collective of like-minded Tigrayans in the diaspora advocating for justice, peace, and the restoration of the constitutional rights of the Tigrayan people. Our mission is grounded in an unwavering commitment to truth, democracy, human dignity, and territorial integrity. Who We Are

We are independent Tigrayan voices united by shared values of justice, democratic governance, and resistance to authoritarianism. Through peaceful advocacy, civic engagement, and strategic diplomacy, we promote sustainable justice and accountability.
