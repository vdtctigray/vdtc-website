Our Mission

VDTC is a collective of like-minded Tigrayans in the diaspora advocating for justice, peace, and the restoration of the constitutional rights of the Tigrayan people.

Core Objectives

  • Pursue justice for genocide and war crimes
  • Resist authoritarianism and promote rule of law
  • Restore Tigray’s full constitutional territory
  • Advocate for displaced persons and community dignity
