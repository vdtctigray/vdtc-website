Our Mission

VDTC is a collective of like-minded Tigrayans in the diaspora advocating for justice, peace, and the restoration of the constitutional rights of the Tigrayan people. Our mission is grounded in an unwavering commitment to truth, democracy, human dignity, and territorial integrity. We are not affiliated with any political party, organization, or government—our commitment is solely to the people of Tigray and their future. VDTC actively engages in diplomatic outreach to support the people of Tigray and serves as a bridge between the diaspora and those on the ground. Through letters, public statements, and direct dialogue with local leaders, we ensure that our efforts reflect the real needs and priorities of Tigrayans. Our focus is change, stability, peace, and justice.

Who We Are

We are independent Tigrayan voices united by shared values of justice, democratic governance, and resistance to authoritarianism. In response to genocide, displacement, and systematic marginalization, we work to amplify the needs and aspirations of the Tigrayan people. Through peaceful advocacy, civic engagement, and strategic diplomacy, we promote sustainable justice and accountability. VDTC is a working group committed to non-political, non-partisan, people-driven action for long-term peace and restoration in Tigray.

Our Core Objectives

  • Pursue justice for genocide and war crimes committed against the Tigrayan people
  • Resist authoritarianism and promote rule of law and democratic institutions
  • Restore Tigray’s full constitutional territory
  • Advocate for the safe, voluntary return of all internally displaced persons (IDPs)
  • Protect civilians from war, displacement, and systemic suffering
  • Promote civic unity among Tigrayans and support diaspora engagement
  • Engage in diplomatic efforts to elevate the concerns of Tigrayans and influence global awareness and policy
