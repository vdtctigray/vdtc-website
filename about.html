About VDTC

Voices of Diaspora Tigray for Change (VDTC) is a global collective of Tigrayan professionals, students, and human rights advocates. We are united by our shared mission to seek justice, promote peace, and restore constitutional rights to the people of Tigray.

Our Founding Values

  • Non-political and people-centered
  • Truth-driven documentation
  • Global solidarity through advocacy
