Who We Are

Voices of Diaspora Tigray for Change (VDTC) is a collective of Tigrayan professionals, students, and advocates living across the global diaspora. We are united by a shared commitment to justice, truth, and the protection of Tigray’s people, identity, and rights.

We are a grassroots, volunteer-based initiative. We are not affiliated with any political party or government. Our focus is people-centered — we speak for civilians, victims, and displaced communities whose voices have been silenced or ignored.

Our Values

  • Justice: We advocate for accountability and recognition of atrocities committed against the Tigrayan people.
  • Democracy: We believe in self-determination, free expression, and constitutional governance.
  • Unity: We promote dialogue and solidarity across the global Tigrayan diaspora.
  • Truth: We oppose propaganda and distortion; we stand for evidence-based documentation and communication.
  • Independence: We remain unaffiliated and unaligned with any political group or armed movement.
